BLOOD IDO

with Muon Network...

/

The Blood IDO will go live on Muon's launch pad using a fair launch structure that benefits existing FEAR Holders.

  • Jan 1 - 14: Wallet Scan Period - FEAR Holders with an ERC 20 wallet (outside of exchanges and staking pools) on Ethereum, Polygon and BSC will have their wallet scanned daily at midnight UTC.
  • Jan 15: Blood Allocation Calculation - The average running balance of each wallet will be calculated during this period. For details of exactly how your Blood allocation is calculated please see the IDO Formula page.
  • Jan 15 (6am UTC): Commit USDC - FEAR holders access a webpage where they can commit USDC for an assigned allocation of Blood.
  • Jan 16 (6am UTC): FEAR Free for All - Any Blood allocation that remains unclaimed will be available on a first come first serve to FEAR Holders only.
  • Jan 17 (6am UTC): Public Sale - Any Blood allocation that remains unclaimed will be available on a first come first serve to the general public.
  • Jan 18 (11am UTC): Claim Day - IDO Participants can claim 20% of their Blood allocation with the remaining 80% vested (see tokenomics page).
  • Jan 19 (11am UTC): Listing Day - Blood Lists on Pancake and Uniswap (Polygon).
  • *** All proceeds from the sale are strictly for liquidity purposes only and no proceeds go to the FEAR team or any private investors or partners. ***

Retaining your remaining 80% Allocation

In order to prevent people trading in and out of FEAR so as to aquire blood your remaining 80% Blood allocation is dependent on you maintaining the average FEAR balance you have going into the IDO.

e.g. if your average running balance of FEAR between the 1st-14th of Jan is 100 FEAR this must be maintained each day in order for your remaining 80% BLOOD allocation to be claimable
