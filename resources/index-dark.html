Premium Token

Blood, Sweat & Gaming

Unlike other in Game Rewarder Tokens Blood cannot be earnt by simply partaking in gameplay. Players must invest time and energy into FEAR games and compete against other players in order to obtain Blood.<;p>

Blood Tech.

Cross Chain Tech

Blood is a Chain Agnostic token utilising Muon's cross chain technology initially and will run on the Polygon & BSC. This is important as both chains have a large number of users.

Tokenomics

15m initial circ supply

Blood will launch with an initial circulating supply of 15 million and a base diluted supply of 30 million. Crucially Blood can also be burnt through the purchase of NFTs and other FEAR digital content.
